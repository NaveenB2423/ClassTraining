It is a necessity of man. You can judge one’s character, taste and habits by the dress he wears. They are the stages of the wearer.

The fashion in dress has changed many times. In the olden days in India man used to wear dhoti, kurta or shirt and women sarees or lehnga. Then came pant-shirt for man and salwar suits for women.
